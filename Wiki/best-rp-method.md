# Best Beginner and Advanced RP Method

## Best Beginner Rp Method

## Requirements
- 6x Advanced Triangle Planter Box III (Maximum you can place at all prestiges)
- 9-12x Advanced Large Planter Box T3 (Prestige 0 starts with 9 Planters, with Prestige 3 you reach the maximum of 12 Planters)
 - A set of 6 triangular and 9 large planters costs 18,000 RP, that you can by in the ingame Shop /s Shop/Buyables.
- Harvesting Skill Tree Perks: 
  - 5x Amature Farmer (adds 37,5% harvesting yield)
  - 5x Expert Farmer (adds another 50% harvesting yield to a total of 87,5%)
  - 1x Ultimate Botanist (this perks allows to set the genes of a seed, but only one seed not every seed you have in your inventory!)
  - You need at least 25 skillpoints to unlock the ultimate perk, so choose your perks as you see fit to unlock the ultimate.
- 600+ Hemp seeds (or just buy a god seed from a shop if somebody offers one. GGGYYY or YYYYYY are god seeds!)
- Reaping Spade
- Fertile Bone
- Full Farming Set
- Mushroom soups (1-2x depends on other skills and the ammount of planters you have placed)
- 100k+ bone fragments
You don't need everything at once to start your RP Farm, the most necessary requirements are the planters and the god seed. Everything else will boost the profit. 

## Method (step-by-step)
1. Wait for night-time (use /tod to check sunset).
 Night lasts about 4 minutes.
2. Consume Mushroom Soup to increase farming yield. (+50% Farming Yield)
3. Use the Reaping Spade on the center planter of your 3x3 setup to harvest all crops instantly.
4. Immediately replant the seeds and use Fertile Bone to skip growth.
5. Repeat the process till night ends.

## Converting to RP
1. After night is over, check cloth prices by using /pricecheck cloth.
2. Move to the server that has the highest rates.
3. Sell the cloth (/sell) in the world closest to ~2x RP.

This method is currently one of the fastest and most consistent RP farming strategies on Brits PvE Worlds, especially when combined with a full farming setup and large seed stock.
TIP: You can either farm the bones by using a Hunter Full Set with a Baloo Knife (and a bear pie together with a super serum) or directly buy them by going to the /hub then opening the shop (/s) in the stock market section and looking for bone fragments.

Buy them at a rate of 10/15 RP per, higher than that will not make you have any profits or barely any.

## Advanced Rp Method 1 - Sheet Metal
## Requirements: 
- Scrap (100k to start is enough, but more scrap = more profit)

## How to get Scrap: 
### Selling Plants
- You can plant roses, sunflowers or orchids with the same method as above. 
- Sell ​​your plants at the outpost for scrap; it doesn't matter which flower you sell, they are all worth the same. (10x Flower = 15 Scrap)
### Buying Scrap from other players
- Buy Scrap from other players with vending machines or in the /s market at the hub. 
 - This method yields the largest quantity of scrap, but the lowest profit. Only buy scrap at a price that allows you to make a profit. You can easily calculate that yourself but I'll give a example in the step-by-step method.

## Sheet Metal Method (step-by-step)
- Buy Sheet Metal from Outpost at a maximum rate of 50 scrap per Sheet Metal.
  - If you use scrap that you bought from other players, the maximum price will decrease accordingly!
  - A simple calculation; Sheet Metal buying Price (for example 30) times Scrap Buying Rate (for example 1.5) = Final Sheet Metal buying price. Now Sheet Metal selling price (for example 70) dividet by Buying price = profit per 1 scrap. 30x1.5=45 70/45= 1.55 RP per 1 scrap! A counter-invoice would be 70/30=Result /1,5 = 1.55.
  - If you only use scrap from plants you can just divide the selling price through the buying price.
- Sell the Sheet Metal for a higher price, highest price is 70.
- Be aware Sheet Metal only stacks to 1k! But if you use the drones you can stack it up to 9999. 
- You can't use /pricecheck on sheet metal, so you need to look through all the worlds and raid worlds for the highest selling price!

## Advanced RP Method 2 - Tactical Gloves
This is a method for starting the game and can be used from level 1 to achieve the maximum RP yield per scrap, which equates to a profit of approximately 2400%. It is not intended for long-term use, as it takes too long even for a clan.

## Requirements:
- Scrap (100 is enough to start)
- Recycler (Monument! or your own from /s)
   - Don't recylce at outpost or bandit camp!

## Tactical Gloves Method (step-by-step)
- Buy Tactical Gloves from outpost. Best price is 20, worst price is around 80 Scrap.
  - You can only buy one Glove per click! Thats why it isn't good enough for a long-term use, because it's way to time consuming.
- Recycle the glove and every component that u get out of it.
  - You should get 288 Cloth and 12 Leather out of one glove!
- Sell the Cloth with /pricecheck cloth to the world with the highest selling price. (2 is the highest selling price).
- Sell the Leather (no need for looking around each world, it's just a byproduct and won't bring you that much profit).

## 2400% Profit??? 
Here is the calculation:
- Tactical Gloves for the best price at 20. Gloth for the best selling price of 2. You get 288 gloth out of one glove and 12 leather. 288x2=576 + selling value of leather for example 20 = 596 RP
- 596 RP divided by 20 Scrap = 29,8 RP per Scrap.
 - Procentage Calculation: 29,8*100=2980%
- Because you can't buy the gloves always for the best price I took 2400%.
